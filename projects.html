Project 01

📞 Cold Calling Toolkit & Lead Generation System

Designed and developed a complete outbound sales toolkit from scratch for targeting small and medium businesses in Canada. The project involved building customized cold calling scripts for VoIP services (eFone) and residential phone plans (Homephone), along with objection handling guides, voicemail templates, and a structured follow-up workflow. Lead lists were built using browser-based scraping tools to pull business data from YellowPages.ca and Kijiji. The system reduced manual prospecting time significantly and improved call-to-lead conversion.

Technologies & Tools Used:

  • Instant Data Scraper
  • ExtensionsGod Yellow Pages Scraper
  • YellowPages.ca
  • Kijiji
  • Microsoft Excel (CRM Tracking)
  • VoIP Dialer
  • Google Docs (Script Writing)
Project 02

🚚 Remote Freight Dispatching Business Plan

Researched, planned, and drafted a complete business model for launching a remote freight dispatching service targeting owner-operators and small carriers in the United States. The project covered regulatory requirements, dispatcher–carrier agreement templates, load board usage strategies (DAT and 123Loadboard), pricing structures, and a carrier acquisition plan using Facebook groups. The plan also included a startup cost analysis showing near-zero overhead for a remote operation based in Pakistan.

Technologies & Tools Used:

  • DAT Load Board
  • 123Loadboard
  • Facebook Groups (Carrier Outreach)
  • Microsoft Word (Documentation)
  • Microsoft Excel (Financial Planning)
  • Google Research
